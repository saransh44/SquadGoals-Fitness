What We Do

Duet helps stay-at-home parents develop healthy lifestyle habits. With Duet, you can finally publish your dream blog, achieve your fitness goals, and read every night. Duet makes habit-building fun, easy, and rewarding. Sometimes, being a homemaker can get lonely. Duet allows you to develop healthy habits while forging a friendship with an accountability partner. You and your partner help each other through the habit-building journey, developing a priceless relationship in the process.

Features

Set Achievable Habits Get Connected Build Lasting Friendships
You can either select a pre-made habit template, or create your own. Duet makes sure you build your habit incrementally, starting small but achieving lasting success down the road. With Duet, you build habits in pairs using the proven habit-building technique of social accountability. We connect you to an Accountability Partner who is also trying to build a habit. At the end of each day, you message your partner, telling them how much progress you made on your habit. Duet is more than just a habit-building application. If you want, you can truly get to know your Accountability Partner and eventually become friends with them, making your time on the app more fun, and more rewarding.
